What is CardionAI?
CardionAI is an integrated heart-health platform designed to monitor vital signs, analyze medical data, predict potential risks, and provide instant communication between patients and doctors. Using AI-driven models, CardionAI tracks heart performance trends and alerts both the patient and the physician when something requires attention.
